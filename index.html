SpaceX designs, manufactures and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

Falcon9

Falcon 9 is a rocket designed and built by SpaceX. It is used to transport Dragon spacecraft and satellites into orbit.

Falcon Heavy

Falcon Heavy is the most powerful operational rocket in the world, With the ability to lift nearly 64 metric tons.

Dragon Capsule

Dragon is a free-flying spacecraft built to transport cargo and people to space. It can even return cargo back to earth.

Reusable Vehicles

Spacex is using reusable rocket which are very important in reducing cost of Space journey. The rockets are used again.
